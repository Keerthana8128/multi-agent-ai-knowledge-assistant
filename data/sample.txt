Our company is planning to launch an AI knowledge assistant for internal teams.
The assistant should help employees quickly summarize documents, answer questions, and recommend next steps.

The first release will focus on document ingestion, retrieval, summarization, and question answering.
The system should be easy to use and should reduce time spent searching through internal documents.

Important goals:
- Improve employee productivity
- Reduce manual searching time
- Provide accurate summaries
- Suggest clear next actions after answering questions
